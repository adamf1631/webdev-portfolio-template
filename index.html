About Me:

Adam has been sketching and creating artwork since Elementary school. He comes from a long line of graphic artists. His Grandfather, a graphic artist for the Philadelphia Inquirer for 48 years. His father, John, has been a graphic artist/editor for close to 35 years. As well as his younger brother and cousins who are in the art field.
Adam made the switch from pencil and paper to computer in High School where he began using programs like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator and Quarkxpress. He made numerous designs for his friend's music albums as well as logos and designs for family members.
After years of amateur work Adam wanted to make his talent a professional one, so he enrolled in the Web Design program at the Art Institute and began his schooling. Adam has two children: Sakura and Rodolfo "Rudy."
